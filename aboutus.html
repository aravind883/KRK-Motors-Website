About Us

Welcome to KRK Honda

KRK Motors is a Two-wheeler automobile dealership under Honda Motorcycles and scooters India Pvt. Ltd. started by people who believe that every customer deserves the opportunity to shop for the perfect motorcycle, obtain the right accessories, and receive excellent service in a friendly environment.

Over 15K+ happy customers use our products because our vehicles not only eliminate the need to rely on public transport but also helps you to reach your destination faster thereby reducing a significant amount of time. Today, we have a strong focus on expanding our products to a wide range of customers

We expanded our network to be able to provide more than sales of new vehicles. We are offering a 4S system for our customers to enlighten them in all aspects. From the selection of our vehicles till its lifetime, we are making sure people who join us gain a hassle-free experience.

On top of that, we have always been happy to give back to the open-source community and contribute to its evolution and growth. We created a dedicated page, where you can have a glimpse of our products and services.

We are proud to say that for the last 7 years, we worked at the forefront of sales to bring the best on the table. We genuinely embrace our mission to help people have a positive impact they are part of, and we do that through the products we develop, the way we craft experiences for our customers, and the endeavours we lean in. Behind every great human achieving, there is a team, Our mission is to unleash the potential of every team who works in this.
